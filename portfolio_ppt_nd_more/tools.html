Canva

Very easy to use graphic design app that is accessible for everyone.

  • Completely free for personal use.
  • Automatically storing your work online.
  • Many free templates, vector images, fonts, and layouts to use.
  • Quick import and export of images.
  • You will have the option to upgrade to premium version.

    • Cons:
  • Your efficiency will highly depend on your internet speed.
  • Not very compatible with professional graphic design software (e.g. photoshop, AI).
  • Some of the elements are for premuim only.

    Pixlr

    Professional photo and graphic editor launched with an web app.

  • Completely free for personal use.
  • You can choose to store your work-in-progress online or to local.
  • Functions are very similar to Photoshop.
  • Quick import and export of images.
  • Relatively compatible with Photoshop, AI and Creative Cloud.

    • Cons:
  • Still, your efficiency will highly depend on your internet speed.
  • Limited export format.
  • Annoying banner ads!
